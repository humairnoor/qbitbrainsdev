HPPD Subtype

Some research has suggested that there may be two HPPD subtypes. Please read the following and select whether you believe one or the other is more consistent with your experience:

Type 1 HPPD is characterized by brief "flashbacks" that come on with unpredictable timing and usually resolve completely between episodes.

Type 2 HPPD is marked by long-term, pervasive perceptual disturbances that persist continuously or almost continuously. HPPD-2 tends to be more debilitating, often interfering with daily functioning and mental well-being.
